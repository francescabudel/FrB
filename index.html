About me.

Hey there, I am Francesca Budel.
If you are here, you are probably interested in my personal and professional journey through the world of data, technology and humanities.
Well, this is the right place: in the following sections you will find all about my skills, projects and interests!
I define myself as writer and journalist turned data analyst and digital storyteller.
With a Bachelor's in Literature and Languages and a Master's in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge, I've got a unique perspective on data, coding, and culture.
To me, working with data means working with stories and identities, in a process that always pursues creativity.

Skills

My background allows me to combine different sets of skills: humanities shaped my creativity, my researching ability and my communicative talents, while computer science allowed me to interact and shape information through coding.

Data Analysis

My proficiency in Data Analysis extends from data cleaning and dataset alignment to machine learning algorithms and in-depth explorations.
Among my most used languages and tools, I can list Python, R and SQL, that I've consistently used for data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and hypothesis testing.
Whether it's identifying trends, detecting anomalies, or segmenting customer behavior, I have a proven track record of transforming raw data into actionable intelligence.

In my latest projects, I analysed huge datasets containing information about users' preferences, behaviours and habits to find meaningful trends and patterns in order to understand a much more complex scenario.

Machine Learning

I have entered the world of Machine Learning to give even more depth and solidity to my analysis: through the application of predictive language models, linear and logistic regression, neural networks and random forests I have successfully measured and explored the impact of sexist language in online movie reviews.
To do this, I have employed libraries and frameworks like PyTorch, scikit-learn and TensorFlow.

Information Visualization

I have fallen in love with Information Visualization in the scope of digital storytelling: I truly believe that intuitive visual representation can turn the most difficult and complex topic in an approachable and effective narrative.
I mostly work with Tableau, Flourish, Seaborn, Matplotlib and Chart.js, as many of my project showcase.

Curiosity and Learning

I am an eager learner and I am always ready to challenge myself. I actively engage in research and educational pursuits to enhance my skills and knowledge.
My work as a journalist, moreover, encourages me to inquire and explore different fields of knowledge: every topic, from technology to mainstream news, passing through psychology, culture and politics, can enrich one's narrative or perspective.
Curiosity without prejudice enriches me as an individual and as a professional.

Digital Storytelling

From conceptualization to execution, I immerse myself in the storytelling process, ensuring that each narrative arc is thoughtfully crafted to evoke emotions, provoke thoughts, and spark conversations.
Through meticulous attention to detail and a deep understanding of narrative structures and my writing career, I create experiences that leave a lasting impact on viewers and that encourage interaction, reflection and empathy.

Creativity

Creativity is at the heart of everything I do.
I thrive in environments that encourage experimentation, innovation, and pushing the boundaries of conventional thinking.
Whether it's brainstorming ideas for a new project, designing visual assets, or solving creative challenges, I bring a fresh perspective and a relentless pursuit of excellence to every endeavor.
In all of my projects, I have offered unique perspectives and creative strategies to explore data and create stories.
