About me.

Hey there, I am Francesca Budel. If you are here, you are probably interested in my personal and professional journey through the world of data, technology and humanities. Well, this is the right place: in the following sections you will find all about my skills, projects and interests! I define myself as writer and journalist turned data analyst and digital storyteller.
With a Bachelor's in Literature and Languages and a Master's in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge, I've got a unique perspective on data, coding, and culture.
To me, working with data means working with stories and identities, in a process that always seeks creativity.
Explore my portfolio to discover more, and, if we are like-minded, let's get in touch!
